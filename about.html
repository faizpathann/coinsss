About NumisCoin

Faizan Pathan — A Journey from Dhar to the World of Numismatics My name is Faizan Pathan, and I hail from Dhar, Madhya Pradesh. Since childhood, I’ve been deeply fascinated by coins and currency notes. While other kids were drawn to toys and games, I found myself captivated by the stories etched into old coins and the designs printed on faded notes. Each piece felt like a portal to another time — a silent witness to history. What began as a simple hobby soon grew into a lifelong passion. I started collecting not just for the sake of possession, but to understand the art, culture, and legacy behind each item. For me, numismatics is not just about collecting — it’s about preserving heritage, discovering forgotten tales, and connecting with the past in a tangible way. This passion inspired me to create a platform where fellow collectors can come together — a trusted space to buy, sell, and exchange coins and notes. But more than that, it’s a community built on shared curiosity and respect for history. Through this website, I aim to support enthusiasts who, like me, see more than just metal and paper — they see stories, craftsmanship, and identity. This isn’t just a business. It’s a dream born from childhood wonder, shaped by years of dedication, and driven by a heartfelt desire to keep history alive — one coin at a time.
